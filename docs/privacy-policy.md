**Effective Date:** December 16, 2025

## Introduction

HealTone ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the HealTone mobile application (the "App"). 

By using HealTone, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** (for account creation and authentication)
- **Profile information** you voluntarily provide
- **Authentication tokens** (for secure access)

### Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- **App usage statistics** (features used, session duration)
- **Audio preferences** (favorite frequencies, custom bath configurations)
- **Progress tracking data** (manifestation streaks, session completion)
- **Journal entries** (mood tracking, personal notes you choose to save)
- **Device information** (device type, operating system version)
- **Performance data** (crash reports, error logs for app improvement)

### Audio and Content Data
- **Custom bath compositions** you create
- **Frequency preferences and favorites**
- **Session history and timing preferences**
- **Cached audio files** (stored locally on your device for offline use)

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

### Primary Functions
- **Provide core app functionality** (audio playback, user authentication)
- **Sync your data** across devices you're logged into
- **Personalize your experience** (recommendations, favorite frequencies)
- **Enable offline access** to your preferred content

### App Improvement
- **Analyze usage patterns** to improve app performance
- **Fix bugs and crashes** through error reporting
- **Develop new features** based on user engagement data
- **Optimize audio quality** and streaming performance

### Communication
- **Send important updates** about the app or service changes
- **Provide customer support** when you contact us
- **Share wellness tips** (only if you opt-in to notifications)

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- **Audio cache** is stored locally on your device for offline access
- **User preferences** are stored locally and synced to our secure servers
- **Journal entries** can be stored both locally and on secure cloud servers

### Cloud Storage
- We use **Supabase** (a secure, compliant database service) to store:
  - User account information
  - Synchronized preferences and favorites
  - Progress tracking data
  - Custom bath configurations

### Security Measures
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Authentication**: Secure login systems protect your account
- **Access Controls**: Limited employee access on a need-to-know basis
- **Regular Audits**: Security practices are regularly reviewed and updated

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### Access and Control
- **View your data**: Access your profile, favorites, and journal entries in-app
- **Edit information**: Modify your profile and preferences anytime
- **Export data**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Delete specific data**: Remove individual journal entries, custom baths, or favorites

### Account Management
- **Account deletion**: Permanently delete your account and associated data
